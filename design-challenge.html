My Design Challenge

The Problem I am trying to solve is...

I want to make known the joy of making art to kids for thier fun but also to bring them and the world more joy from creating.

1) Take a stab at framing it as a design question.
How can i show and give art resources to kids for their joy.

2) Now state the ultimate impact your tyring to have.
I want young kids to be happy from art and creativity.

3) What are some posible solutions to your problem?
More happiness, brain growth, and early childhood prblem solving solutions.

4) Write down some of the context and constrains that your facing.
Not being able to reach the kids geographically when they might appreicate it the most.

5) Does your original question need a tweak?
How can i give and show parens how to give resources to their kids to show joy.
