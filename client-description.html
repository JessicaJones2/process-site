Client Description

Below I have stated my client and some of how they have impacted others over the years.

International Child Art Foundation

My client is a company called the International Child Art Foundation. Their vision is to inspire children to become future leaders with crative minds and empathetic hearts. ICAF put together a Art Olympic every four years that show cases both the creativiy and physical movement for for children of the ages eight to twelve around the world.

Founded in 1997 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, the International Child Art Foundation (ICAF) serves American schoolchildren as their national arts organization that cultivates creativity and mutual empathy worldwide through the universal language of art. ICAF calls for students to identify as creative empaths so they can work together to improve the world. Developing self-identity shapes personality and character and directs one's thoughts and life purpose. Developing cosmic Identity IS a more difficult task, requiring a holistic education that integrates STEM disciplines with art for imagination and sports for teamwork. ICAF uses STEAM education in its programs and its quarterly magazine, ChildArt, tor CroSS-cultural understanding, CrosS-diSCiplinary thinking, and global competencies

Impact

Approximately 5 million children in 80 countries have produced origional workd under the ICAF programs. An estimated 2,500,000 individuals have participated in or attendd ICAF's festivals and exhibition that have taken place in over a dozen major cities worldwide. Thousands of children learn about ICAF every week, gaining confidenc as creators and changemakers.
